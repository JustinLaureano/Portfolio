The Gathering


The Gathering is the perfect venue to host a bridal shower, baby shower, board meeting, or a special birthday party. Our beautifully renovated historic building offers a unique setting and will surely set your event above the rest.
You and your party can enjoy the open kitchen atmosphere and allow our top notch kitchen and service team to take care of the rest.

The Gathering Wine


The Gathering Wine has been host to many private events, from rehearsal dinner’s to private corporate functions, we will set your event above the rest. The open layout of Gathering Wine allows us to accomodate any and all types of functions. With granite tables and a glass enclosed wine cellar, Gathering Wine is quite easy on the eyes.

The Anvil


Anvil Whiskey Bar is the ultimate spot for private events in Findlay, Ohio. Wow your guests with the largest selection of Bourbon and Whiskey in town, and take advantage of the beautiful balcony view! Anvil Whiskey Bar features exposed brick and many historical nuances, which provides one of the most unique settings for your next corporate function, or birthday party!

The Patio


The courtyard setting offers a secluded place to unwind and features two large murals by local artist, Oscar Velasquez. With live entertainment and an outdoor grill, the patio provides the perfect retreat from hustle and bustle of downtown. Our Patio is also the best location to host your next rehearsal dinner, bridal shower, or fundraising event. It’s seclusion is what sets it apart from any other outdoor event location. From our freshly planted flowers and herbs, and upon sunset, the perfect muted light given off by our beautifully strung lights from above, we are confident that our Patio is the only place you should plan your next outdoor function.
