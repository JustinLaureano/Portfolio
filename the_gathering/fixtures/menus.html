Wine List

    • Red Wine

    • Matchbook, “The Arsonist”, California, 2013

      2 - Taste
      8 - Glass

      52% Petit Verdot, 24% Cabernet Sauvignon, and 24% Merlot. The wine shows notes of raspberry jam, milk chocolate, new leather, and a slight herbal tone with a med-full body and a moderate finish.

    • Tenet, “The Pundit”, Syrah, Columbia Valley, Washington, 2014

      2 - Taste
      9 - Glass

      Ripe cherry and plum aromas are complemented by earthy undercurrents of smoky cocoa and cola spice. A silky finish with flavors of red cherry, blackberry, and hints of toasted vanilla.

    • Vignali Roccamora, Nero d’Avola, Sicily, Italy, 2013

      2 - Taste
      7 - Glass

      The wine is dark ruby in color and shows aromas of red berries, fig spice, rose petal, pepper, and earth. The wine is medium-full bodied and has a very soft texture mid-palate closing with firm tannins.

    • Massaya, “Silver Selection,” Bekaa Valley, Lebanon, 2011

      2 - Taste
      9 - Glass

      A blend of 40% Grenache, 30% Cinsault, 15% Mourvedre, and 15% Cabernet Sauvignon. Earthy, saturated plum fruit with exotic spices, game, and medicine kit nuances.

    • Michael David, “Freakshow”, Cabernet Sauvignon, Lodi, California, 2013

      2 - Taste
      9 - Glass

      Made from 100% Cabernet Sauvignon grown in the backwaters of Lodi, this dense, purple-colored wine possesses plenty of crème de cassis, vanilla, spice box and cedar notes in a fruit-bomb style.

    • Concha Y Toro, Gran Reserva, Malbec, Marchigue, Chile, 2014

      2 - Taste
      7 - Glass

      A rich concentration of dark fruit with accents of chocolate and spice with sweet, chewy tannins.

    • Ponzi, Pinot Noir, Tavola, Willamette Valley, Oregon, 2015

      2 - Taste
      9 - Glass

      Fresh and inviting with cherry and raspberry notes at the core, showing hints of cinnamon and hibiscus around the edges.

    • Ca’ Montebello, Sangue di Giuda, Lombardy, Italy, 2014

      2 - Taste
      6 - Glass

      This sweet red has loads of strawberry, raspberry, and blueberry jam notes and a slight effervescence.

    • Muga, Reserva, Red Blend, Rioja, Spain, 2010

      3 - Taste
      10 - Glass

      70% Tempranillo, 20% Garnacha, 7% Mazuelo, and 3% Graciano. The wine is dense and deep with black raspberry, mocha, vanilla, and smoky oak nuances. It finishes long with persistent tannins.

    • Domaine Chaume-Arnaud, Red Blend, Vinsobres, Rhone, France, 2013

      2 - Taste
      7 - Glass

      Classic southern Rhone in style with juicy dark berries, smoky plum, mineral, and meaty notes on the nose. The wine is medium bodied and is a blend of Grenache, Syrah, Cinsault, & Mourvedre.

    • Juan Gil, Monastrell, Jumilla, Spain, 2014

      2 - Taste
      7 - Glass

      Rich concentration of dark berries with accents of cracked pepper, cured meat, and toasty vanilla.

    • White Wine

    • Kraft, Chenin Blanc, Vouvray, France, 2015

      Taste - 2
      Glass - 7

      Slightly off-dry on the finish. A rich tropical nose belies the drier palate. Baked pear, kiwi, lime and flowers on a fruity mid-palate followed by a beautifully acidic finish showing a hint of marzipan.

    • Terradora Dipaolo, Fiano di Avellino, Italy, 2015

      2 - Taste
      8 - Glass

      Quince, apricot, hazelnut, spice, and orange blossoms on the nose with good weight and texture.

    • Pfeffingen, Pinot Noir “Blanc de Noir”, Pfalz, Germany, 2016

      2 - Taste
      7 - Glass

      Yes, this is a white wine made from red grapes. Please ask us how this is possible! Bright fruit flavors of peach and white cherry with refreshing acidity and a dry finish.

    • Sbragia, Chardonnay, “Home Ranch”, Dry Creek Valley, California, 2014

      3 - Taste
      10 - Glass

      Wonderful aromas of ripe apples, tropical fruit and toasted almonds with a buttery brioche note. On the nose there is vanilla and baking spice from the oak.

    • Dagueneau, “Les Pentes”, Sauvignon Blanc, Pouilly-Fumé, France, 2015

      3 - Taste
      10 - Glass

      Classic in style with green apple, crushed rock, subtle grapefruit and cut grass nuances with a medium body and lively, crisp acidity on the finish. .

    • Marenco, Strev, Moscato d’Asti, Italy, 2016

      2 - Taste
      6 - Glass

      Slightly sparkling and sweet with notes of pears, oranges, and white flowers.

*All bottles are priced at state minimum for retail (to go) purchase. Any bottle consumed on premise will be sold at retail plus $10.
