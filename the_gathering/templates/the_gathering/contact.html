The Gathering


Offered at each of the Gathering properties, our Chef’s unique recipes provide guests an exceptional culinary experience at an affordable price. From the best burger and soups in town for lunch, to remarkable handcrafted appetizers and dinner selections, to made-from-scratch desserts, The Gathering is truly a 5-star dining experience.

The Gathering Wine


Gathering Wine, located at 235 S. Main St. in downtown Findlay, is the city’s finest retail wine shop and wine bar featuring over six-hundred wines from around the world. Gathering Wine features a rotating menu of seventeen wines by the glass, or create your own flight of tasting portions as you relax in the upscale atmosphere of the bar, or adjourn to the patio and enjoy the outdoors.
